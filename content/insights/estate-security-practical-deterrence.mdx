Estate security improves when routines are predictable, visible, and documented.

## Start with the gate

The gate is the first control point. A disciplined process includes identity checks, clear visitor logging, and escalation for non-compliant entry attempts.

## Visibility matters

Visible presence in common zones and at access points deters opportunistic behavior. Patrol timing should be structured, not random, so teams can prove route completion.

## Reporting builds control

Incident and shift reporting gives management the information needed to refine staffing, update post orders, and maintain accountability.

## Keep procedures current

As resident traffic or contractor flow changes, post orders should be reviewed and adjusted.
