Farm and rural sites face long perimeters, changing activity windows, and limited immediate oversight.

## Prioritize access points

Start with controlled access at primary entry routes. Record visitors, deliveries, and unusual movement patterns.

## Define patrol zones

Large properties need patrol zones based on risk, not convenience. Patrol schedules should focus on vulnerable boundaries and after-hours windows.

## Set escalation contacts early

Escalation should be pre-agreed with owners or managers. Fast communication reduces confusion during incidents.

## Use practical reporting

Reports should document patrol completion, anomalies, and response actions in plain language.
